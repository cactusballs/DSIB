Don't smoke, it's bad is an SMS service that sens you daily reminders of why quitting smoking is the best thing you can do for yourself. You have the flexibility to set the frequency, time and content of your messages. It's your journey and any reason to quit smoking is a good one. In the sign up form you can input your own messagge, choose our randomiser to receive a rotating selection or have a roll of our magic eight ball and pick the one that suits you.
